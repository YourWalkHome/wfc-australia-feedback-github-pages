Ben Ryan

Founder, WFC Australia

Hi, I'm Ben.

I work with business owners, leaders, and organisations who are carrying important decisions, pressure, complexity, or change.

My role is not to arrive with pre-built answers. It is to understand what is happening, help separate symptoms from root causes, and support practical next steps that fit the organisation.

Before we speak, Compass can help organise your thoughts so our conversation begins from a clearer place.

Compass

WFC Australia's AI conversation guide

Compass helps you prepare for your conversation with Ben. It listens, reflects, and helps organise what feels most important right now.

Compass does not provide advice. Compass does not diagnose problems. Compass does not replace people.

Its role is simple: help you arrive at the conversation feeling understood.

How it works.

  1. Compass helps organise your thoughts.
  2. You review what will be shared.
  3. Ben receives the context and continues the conversation.
